JCUB Book Store

Goals

To develop a responsive website for JCUB students to make it easy for students to purchase used books online with ease from anywhere on Internet.

Objectives of selecting this project :

  1. Student friendly website for buying or selling used books.
  2. Opportunity for students to buy or sell on the same portal
  3. Displaying the books online will help students to select books anytime online avoiding the current scenario where they are given options to select on specific date and time at JCU using Google Form.

Why JCUB Book Store ?

JCUB students do not have an opportunity to Buy/Sell the used books with ease. On creating a website for this purpose, it will help students to look for books from the website and select when necessary online. With the growing increase in number of students at JCUB every trimester, this website will also be useful in the future.

Importance/Novelty of this project :

JCUB online book store will enhance our knowledge on designing a Web-page, documentation using HTML and we will get a hands-on Softwares such as Adobe Dreamweaver, PHP,CSS and usage of HTML tags. Besides, JCUB book store will help students of JCUB to Buy/Sell their used books. Students around JCU can select books from the specify category they want to buy.

Target Users:

Primary Users
- Students of JCU who want to buy or sell books online
Secondary Users
- Staff members of JCU
User Characteristics
- Age / Gender: 13+ / Both male and female
- Education: Secondary school or higher
- Computer and web experience: Online shopping experience

Features:

  • To make buying and selling of JCUB used books online.
  • Enable all JCU students to log inside the portal using their JCU ID and password
  • Enable students to select books depending upon their degree and branch.
  • Providing a comprehensive search module that will help students to view available books across website.
  • Simple payment portal with users havign options to pay Cash or Account transfer to the student bank account who is selling the book. Payments through credit / debit cards or PayPal shall be decided in the future depending upon the response for the site.
  • Working Staff at JCU will also have an access to view current available books.
  • Site will also welcome staff/students who desire to donate books for students.

Features to enhance User Experience:

  • Interactive user interface design
  • Usage of media:
    Images - Book Cover , Buttons , My Cart

Technologies

  • Front end : HTML and CSS
  • Back end : PHP and MySQL
  • IDE : Dreamweaver and Eclipse
  • Main functions : Log in, Log out,Buying, Selling, Searching, Filtering
